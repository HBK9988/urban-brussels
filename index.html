Urban Brussels

Urban Brussels is the bridge between the urban database and the community of Brussels.

Our open web application lists and maps the cultural heritage and history of Brussels in an interactive and user-friendly way.

The project

What? Who? When? Questions you ask wondering around Brussels. Our web application has the answers! It provides you with a mesmorizing experience in discovering Brussels' architectual heritage.

Iris Monument

Iris Monument is an inventory of immovable properties in Brussels. It provides educational insights in architecture and town-planning history. Its goal is to safeguard Brussels' urbanism and serve as a tool for researcher and policymakers.

Through a search travel portal you can discover the architectual sights in different municipalities all over Brussels. Go explore !

Check Iris Monument

Want to know the details?

In case you are interested in a particular building, you can open its detailed page where you will find its unique attributes: typologies, styles, and architect

Play with the data

Using Iris Monument database you can view valueable architectual insights on urbanism in Brussels. Our dashboard provides you with the following statitics: Buildings per Architect, Buildings per Style, Buildings per Typology and the Timeline of Buildings over time. Starting all the way from 1350 !
